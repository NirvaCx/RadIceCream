Open the game by running (double-clicking) RadIceCream.bat

Use the numbers on the top row of your keyboard to navigate the menus

## Note: All controls are lowercase.
Ingame:
W, A, S and D to move
[Space] to perform special
[P] to pause the game

## Cheats:
Level Select:
[0] - Unlock All Levels
[-] - Reset Progression

Ingame:
[0] - Instantly Win
[-] - Instantly Lose
